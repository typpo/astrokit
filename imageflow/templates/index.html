01 What is AstroKit?

AstroKit is a set of tools that enables you to participate in asteroid exploration by contributing data through a web browser.

You can also use it to download data and to produce graphics of asteroid locations. Our vision is professional scientists, citizen scientists, and students all collaborating to build on the existing knowledge of asteroids.

02 How You Can Help Contribute to AstroKit

You can help discover asteroids by sharing images of the objects you see in the night sky.

Each contribution enables us to map the positions of asteroids with greater accuracy. You can also contribute suggestions on the code. Explore our website for more information.
